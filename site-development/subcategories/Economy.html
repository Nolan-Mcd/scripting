There are various categories of work here in Lethbridge, one of the larger economic drivers is the college and university. In terms of the core industries, there’s a lot of agriculture and agri-food businesses surrounding the city. There’s a good amount of supply chain and logistic companies around the area, to help with transporting livestock, machinery, and agricultural products across the country and continent. Technology also contributes to the local economy, allowing companies to thrive and lead their way in various industries that use it such as new media, VR, and health industries. Lethbridge seems to make a good home for smaller and middle-class manufacturing companies, with its diverse manufacturing base that is well supported here in Lethbridge.
